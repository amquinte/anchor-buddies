About Us

Founded and based in the beatiful city of San Diego, CA, our team consist of young, intelligent students of UCSD. We are a driven team looking to rise up in the tech industry.

Here at Anchor Buddies we like to share the burdern and work as closely together as possible. Although we have designated positions, our members are qualified to assist others outside their position. As a result, deadlines are met as the burden of any one particular task does not fall on a single person. Help is always one anchor away.

We also like to promote a positive atmosphere of respect and interpersonal communication. We want our employees to feel comfortable at work so that they can be more productive and feel safe in voicing their opinions. Here at Anchor Buddies, all ideas are welcomed.
